# 1- Headings
how to give headings to markdown files. To give heading we use # symbol before the text and make sure to give the space between # and first word or letter. as you see below, increase the number of #s will decrease the size of heading. Further you can only make 6 heading(only use the 6# symbol.)
# Heading 1
## Heading 2
### Heading 3
#### Heading 4

# 2- Blocks of Words

## How separate the blocks?
To do this, press twice the Enter key.
Additionaly you make the special text by starting with the greater than > sign before the text/line.
>This is my first line of code.

>Write in markdown.
## How to make single block?
To make single block you can separate the line with > sign
>This is my first line of code.
>
>Write in markdown.
